<p><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: verdana, geneva, sans-serif;"><strong>About:</strong></span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12px; font-family: verdana, geneva, sans-serif;">A tech/exploration pack focused on the mods Create and Integrated Dynamics. With these two mods alone, it is possible to automate nearly everything you can think of. If the main mods don't do the trick, a handful of others have been added to provide additional options when needed. Lightweight enough that it doesn't take forever to load, yet still providing a robust modded experience.</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12px;"><strong><span style="font-family: verdana, geneva, sans-serif;">Featuring:</span></strong></span></p>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: 12px; font-family: verdana, geneva, sans-serif;">Many custom recipe and balance changes intended to motivate players to utilize the main mods</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: 12px; font-family: verdana, geneva, sans-serif;">Full quest lines with rewards to guide players through all the content the pack has to offer (WIP)</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: 12px; font-family: verdana, geneva, sans-serif;">40+ quality of life mods to make life a little easier</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: 12px; font-family: verdana, geneva, sans-serif;">Tons of fancy blocks to decorate your homes and factories just how you want</span></li>
</ul>
